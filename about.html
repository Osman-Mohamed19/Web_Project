Our Story

About Safari Trails

How it started

Safari Trails Travel Agency was founded in 2016 by a small group of Nairobi-based guides who had spent years leading game drives in the Maasai Mara and Amboseli for other operators. They kept hearing the same thing from travellers: the parks were unforgettable, but the planning around them — transfers, permits, mismatched itineraries — was not.

We started with a single circuit connecting Nairobi to the Mara, booked entirely through word of mouth. A decade later, we run routes across the Mara, Amboseli, Lake Nakuru and the Diani coast, but the core idea hasn't changed: every trip is planned by people who have actually walked the trail.

Mission & vision

Mission: to design honest, well-paced journeys across Kenya that connect travellers with local guides, conservancies and communities — not just the view from a vehicle window.

Vision: a Kenya where every safari and coastal trip directly supports the people and ecosystems it passes through, and where "off the beaten path" still means something.
