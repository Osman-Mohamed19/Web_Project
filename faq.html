Planning & Booking

We recommend booking at least three months ahead, especially if you plan to travel during the peak migration season (July to October). Popular tented camps in the Mara fill up quickly during this window. For off-peak travel, six to eight weeks is usually enough lead time, though earlier is always better for the best camp selection.

On Safari

The Mara River crossings — the most dramatic part of the migration — typically happen between late July and early October. However, the exact timing shifts each year depending on rainfall patterns in Tanzania's Serengeti. We monitor conditions closely and can advise on the best window for your specific travel dates.

Light, neutral-coloured clothing (khaki, olive, tan) works best — avoid bright colours and white, which attract insects and stand out in the bush. Essentials include a wide-brim hat, sunscreen, insect repellent, a light fleece for early morning drives, and binoculars. A full packing list is sent with every booking confirmation.

All our standard packages use private 4x4 vehicles — you will not be sharing a vehicle with strangers. Each vehicle carries a maximum of six guests. Private vehicles mean your guide can spend as long as you like at any sighting and adapt the route to your interests on the day.

Health & Safety

Yellow fever vaccination is required if you are arriving from a country with risk of transmission. Anti-malaria medication is strongly recommended for safari destinations. We advise consulting a travel health clinic or your doctor at least six weeks before departure for personalised medical advice based on your itinerary and health history.

Travel insurance is not optional — it is a condition of booking with Safari Trails. Your policy must cover emergency medical evacuation, trip cancellation and personal liability. We recommend a policy that specifically covers safari activities and remote area coverage. We can suggest reputable providers if needed.

Coastal Trips

Yes — and we highly recommend it. The most popular combination is three to five days on safari followed by two to three nights at Diani Beach. The contrast works well: the coast gives you time to rest after the early morning game drive schedule. Our Ultimate Kenya package is built around exactly this combination.

For most combined packages we arrange a short domestic flight from Nairobi Wilson Airport to Ukunda airstrip near Diani — the flight takes about an hour. Road transfers to Mombasa or Malindi are also available if you prefer. All inter-destination transfers are included in combined package prices.
